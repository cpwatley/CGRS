Our Program Divisions 

Ruby's House

Ruby House 1, 2 , 3 and 4
Our facilities provide a safe place for women and children with multiple vulnerabilities we promote community, our comprehensive service rich transitional living environments. We implement individual and family case management, setting realistic short term and long-term goals with our clients, tracking progress, 12- step recovery meetings, and relapse prevention groups.
We design our program structure to assist with independent living and household establishment life skills, conflict resolution and effective communication skills building practices.
We provide client advocacy, job preparation, volunteer training, tutoring for our children, and service linkages.
Our services further respond to the academic needs of the children through school registration assistance and the provision of school supplies, backpacks and uniforms

Ruby's Lil Butterflies

Ruby’s Lil’ Butterflies
In many cultures the Butterly symbolizes new beginnings.
The process of transforming from a caterpillar to butterfly is a visual representation of the transformation that takes place when children enter into the presence of Ruby’s Lil’ Butterflies Director Tami Poindexter. Her time spent as CFO of Chosen Gospel Recovery exposed her to the needs of these children on a new scale.
She envisioned a safe place for children to change and grow and Ms. Poindexter carefully curated that space and cultivates these children into their best selves.
The love, care, affirmation and discipline she displays is unmatched.
The future is brighter with Ms. Poindexter taking the time to influence and fuel it by caring for these children by bridging the gap and filling the cracks that they may have otherwise slipped through.
She has been blessed with two biological children and a host of additional blessings that would affirm that there is none like her. Many have attested that their lives are forever changed for the better after interacting with Ms. Poindexter.
Her heart to serve God, has overflowed into her ability to serve children.

Our Mission
