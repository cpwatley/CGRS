Welcome to Faithful Zion First Fruits Ministry

Faithful Zion First Fruits Ministry is a non-Denominational Church established March 2016 Under the Leadership of our Pastor Darlene Watley and Co- Pastor Carolyn "Cookie" Smith.
We are a safe place called church a bible believing church with a heart to serve. Faithful Zion First Fruits Ministry is very passionate about sharing the good news of our Lord Savior Jesus Christ.
We invite you to connect with God to gain a personal relationship with his son our Lord Savior Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the well being and self-suffiency of chronically homeless families and individuals through supportive housing, drug abuse recovery, life skills building, creative communty out-reach and an array of collbaborative services.
