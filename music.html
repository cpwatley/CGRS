Chosen Gospel Recovery Singers

Chosen established a gospel singing ministry that conducts outreach using their vocal skills as a way of connecting with and breaking down barriers to recovery for the hard-to-reach chronic substance abusers.
The goal is to share information with substances abusers about recovery services and to assist them with entering treatment, and attaining and maintaining sobriety.
Chosen's music ministry reduces people's anxiety about outreach workers making initial contact with them. They become more receptive to information that is presented in this non-traditional format.
