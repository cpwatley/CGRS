Chosen Gospel Recovery Inc. (Chosen) is a non-profit 501 C 3 public benefits corporation founded in 2002.
Our mission is to promote the well-being and economic self-sufficiency of chronically homeless families and individuals through supportive housing, drug abuse recovery, life skills building, creative community outreach and an array of collaborative services.
