Our Outreach Services:

During 2005-2006, we contacted over 500 individuals through our street outreach activities. During this same period, Ruby's House I and II provided 85 homeless women, many with 2 or more children, with housing, peer support and referrals for alcohol, drug, domestic violence and HIV related services.
Our outreach geographic area is the South Los Angeles area from El Segundo Blvd. (South boundary) to Vernon Blvd. (North boundary) and Main St (East boundary) to Western Blvd. (West boundary).
Client demographics have been represented as women between the ages of 18 and 66 years; 67% African American, 12% Latino and 5% White.
Approximately 90% were living with alcohol and/or other drug problems including Crack Cocaine and Heroin.
Ruby's House provides comprehensive service rich sober living environments where the programs incorporate individual and family case management, 12-step recovery meetings,life skills, job development training, tutoring for our children and housing placement linkages
